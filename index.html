DIRTBUSTERS Description

The company's name Dirtbusters Sdn Bhd is the name of our firm. Our firm is straightforward, making it easy for customers to recognize and remember us. The name was inspired by ‘GHOSTBUSTERS’ movie because they use some machine to capture ghosts like we capture dust in houses and offices.

We will offer expert cleaning services to guarantee that our clients' houses and offices are clean and neat. The date of business commencement is on 15th October 2023. The rationale for selecting this date is because the period between the registration date and the date of company starting is approximately eight months to prepare to start the business.
